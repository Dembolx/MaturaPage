🖼️ Opis fotografii – schemat

Krok 1 – Opis ogólny: "The photo shows... / In this picture I can see..."

Krok 2 – Szczegóły: "In the foreground... There are... who appear to be... The atmosphere seems..."

Krok 3 – Spekulacja: "This picture might illustrate... / It could be related to..."

Krok 4 – Pytania egzaminatora: Odpowiadaj bezpośrednio. "In my opinion... / That's a good point, however..."
